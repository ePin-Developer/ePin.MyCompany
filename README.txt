  1. Sign Up for ePin at https://www.epin.me, click the Sign Up button on the top right corner
  2. Once Sign Up is completed, sign in to https://openidconnect.epin.me and create "Add New" record
  3. Change the ClientID, ClientSecret and DisplayName in the appsettings.json to match what was created on https://openidconnect.epin.me
  
  If you need help to implement, please contact us at support@epin.me .
